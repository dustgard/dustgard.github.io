Bluehost

Bluehost claims that it has "Everything your website needs from start-up to success and the peace of mind with guaranteed reliability, automated security updates, backup data protection and 24/7 support." They offer the following services:

  • Free domain and site builder
  • 24/7 Support
  • Only $2.95/Month
  • 30-Day money back guarantee

HostPapa

Hostpapa claims that it is a "Smart web solutions for your small business. Get your website, email and business online today!" They offer the following services:

  • Unlimited domains and websites
  • Good for small business
  • Free one on one training session
  • Guarantee 99.9% uptime on all plans

Hostinger

Hostinger claims that it is "Simple and intuitive, easy to setup, getting your website live is as simple as a click of a button, and everything you need - provided in a clear way." They offer the following services:

  • Advertised as lighting fast websites
  • Easy to setup
  • Huge sale at as little as $0.99/Month
  • Start a website with an automatic 1-click WordPress installation

Dusty's Top Pick

All three of the web hosting services offer very similar services. However, HostPapa offers a free one on one training session. This would allow direct support for building a successful website and for someone like me stands out from the other two web hosting services. It is important to have a professional looking website and instead of spending hours on learning the system on your own, the site will teach you the best way to utilize their services.
